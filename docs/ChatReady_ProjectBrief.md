# ChatReady — Project Brief

## Mission Statement

**Help people get better results from AI by making their files clean, structured, and ready to understand.**

ChatReady exists to bridge the gap between messy real-world documents and AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and local LLMs.

We believe people should not have to struggle with broken PDFs, messy formatting, wasted tokens, or unreliable copy-paste to use AI effectively.

---

## Vision

**Make every document AI-ready.**

ChatReady transforms files, webpages, videos, screenshots, spreadsheets, and structured data into clean Markdown optimized for Large Language Models (LLMs).

The product does not replace AI.

It prepares content so AI can work better.

---

## What Problem Are We Solving?

Today, people commonly do this:

```txt
Open PDF
↓
Copy messy text
↓
Paste into ChatGPT
↓
Formatting breaks
↓
AI misses context
↓
Bad answers
```

Problems users experience:

- Broken PDF formatting
- Lost document hierarchy
- Wasted tokens from messy extraction
- Poor readability
- Missing sections or context in AI responses
- Bad copy-paste experiences
- Inconsistent results across AI tools

The experience feels frustrating and unreliable.

---

## Our Core Promise

**Better AI inputs. Fewer tokens. Cleaner structure.**

ChatReady prepares documents for AI by converting them into structured Markdown that is easier for LLMs to understand.

Users receive:

- Cleaner formatting
- Better structure preservation
- Lower token usage
- Easier copy-paste into AI
- Downloadable Markdown
- Better consistency across models

---

## Product Positioning

> **A document preparation tool for AI**

NOT:

- a summarizer
- a note-taking app

Think:
